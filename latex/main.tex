\section{System analysis \& Design}

The previous chapters discussed the fundamental concepts that form the basis of this project, including blockchain technology, smart contracts, decentralized storage, and Soulbound Tokens. Building on these concepts, this chapter presents the design and implementation of the proposed blockchain-base NFTs for academic certificates verification system.\\

The chapter begins by examining the limitations of traditional certificate management and verification methods. It then describes the proposed system architecture, including the integration of smart contracts, IPFS-based storage, and an AI-assisted verification interface. Finally, the operational workflow of the system is presented to illustrate how academic certificates are issued, stored, and verified in a decentralized environment.
