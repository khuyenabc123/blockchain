\subsection{Conclusion}

    This research successfully designed and implemented a blockchain-based academic certificate management platform that combines NFT technology, IPFS decentralized storage, smart contracts, and artificial intelligence assistance.\\

    The proposed system demonstrates how blockchain technology can be applied to academic credential management to improve transparency, integrity, and verification efficiency. By storing certificate metadata on IPFS and recording ownership information on blockchain networks, the platform provides a verifiable and tamper-resistant mechanism for managing academic records. In addition, the integration of an AI-assisted verification interface improves usability by translating technical blockchain information into understandable natural-language explanations.\\
    
    Overall, the results indicate that combining Web2 technologies (React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB) with Web3 infrastructure (Ethereum smart contracts, NFTs, and IPFS) provides a practical foundation for next-generation academic credential verification systems.
